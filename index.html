ABOUT US

CARGOTHRIFT.MNL, founded in 2023, aims to transform fashion and promote sustainability. We offer affordable and eco-friendly pre-loved clothing, emphasizing waste reduction and conscious consumerism. Our platform combines style and sustainability, providing a unique online shopping experience.

At CARGOTHRIFT.MNL, we've created a retail destination that merges the excitement of treasure hunting with the ease of online shopping. Our specialty lies in offering a diverse selection of carefully curated pre-loved items to cater to the various preferences of our customers.
