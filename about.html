OUR STORY

Our story began in 2023 with a vision that aimed to revolutionize the fashion industry while promoting sustainability. At CARGOTHRIFT.MNL, we are committed to curating pre-loved clothing, making fashion accessible and eco-friendly. Our passion lies in reducing waste and empowering conscious consumerism, as we firmly believe in the power of fashion to inspire positive change.

We celebrate personal expression and environmental responsibility, fostering a space where fashion embraces both. Join us in embracing secondhand shopping as a vibrant and exciting avenue for unique fashion choices.

MISSION

Our goal at is to revamp cargo fashion, promote sustainability, and offer a selection of pre-loved cargo pants. Intending to motivate individuals to embrace secondhand shopping and actively lessen their environmental impact, we work to build an accessible platform where sustainability and fashion coexist.

VISION

Our vision is to redefine how people view cargo fashion by becoming the top online store for vintage cargo pants purchases. Our platform gives users the freedom to express their sense of style and make a positive impact on the environment. We work to build a future where sustainable fashion choices are the standard.
